Activities

  1. Swimming


    Enjoy swimming on the private beach or private swimming pool with a swim up bar.

  2. Cycling


    Enjoy cycling on the beack or the forest trail and get to see exotic animal and birds.

  3. Beach Volleyball


    Get a group and play beach volley ball till the sun goes down.

  4. Nature trail walk

    Enjoy a nature walk through the forest accompanied by a guide. Nature walks are scheduled for 6am-9am and 3pm-6pm

    Unwind and relax in our spa, our spa runs from 6am-10pm

    There are many more places to enjoy yourself outside the resort.Just go online and enjoy your day.
